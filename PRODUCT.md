# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People inspecting a TikTok account's repost activity. They arrive with a username, want fast confirmation that data loaded, then spend most of their time scanning the repost history for recognizable creators, topics, dates, and engagement signals.

## Product Purpose

NyxRepost turns a TikTok username into a readable repost analytics view. Success means the repost history is immediately useful, thumbnails load reliably, and supporting charts/tags/authors help explain the list without distracting from it.

## Brand Personality

Dark, sharp, minimal. The interface should feel focused and slightly mysterious, but still practical enough to trust as an analytics tool.

## Anti-references

Do not drift into generic SaaS dashboards, full visual style swaps, oversized decorative cards, or designs that bury repost history below secondary analytics. Avoid changing the established dark/glass visual direction unless the user explicitly asks.

## Design Principles

1. Repost history is the primary workflow.
2. Keep analytics supportive, not louder than the list.
3. Preserve the existing dark, low-glow visual identity.
4. Make scanned data feel trustworthy through clear labels, stable layout, and loaded thumbnails.
5. Prefer compact, readable product UI over decorative redesign.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast on dark surfaces, keyboard-accessible controls, stable responsive behavior, no horizontal overflow, and motion that never blocks content.
